Know Us

The Awami League is one of the major political parties in Bangladesh, known for its significant role in the country's history, especially during the liberation war of 1971. Established in 1949, it has been at the forefront of Bangladesh's political landscape, advocating for democracy, social progress, and national sovereignty.

With a focus on unity and progress, the Awami League has contributed to the development of the nation through various policies and programs aimed at economic growth, education, and healthcare. The party's leadership and commitment have played a key role in shaping modern Bangladesh.
